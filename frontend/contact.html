Common questions

Is PostPageX really free?

Yes — completely free during our early access period. No credit card required, no hidden limits. We'll introduce optional paid plans later for teams and agencies, but the core features will always have a free tier.

How does PostPageX connect to my Facebook Pages?

After you sign up with Google or email, you connect your Facebook Pages inside the dashboard using Facebook's official OAuth login. We request only the specific permissions needed to manage your Pages. You choose exactly which Pages to share access to — we never touch anything you don't explicitly grant.

Which Facebook permissions does PostPageX request?

We request: pages_show_list (to list your pages), pages_manage_posts (to create and schedule posts), publish_video (for video and Reels), pages_read_engagement (for analytics), pages_read_user_content (for comment moderation), and pages_manage_metadata (for webhooks and page info). Full details are on our Privacy Policy page.

How many Facebook Pages can I connect?

During early access, you can connect unlimited Pages. There are no artificial limits while we're in this phase — we want you to get the full value of the platform and give us real feedback on how it performs at scale.

Can I bulk upload posts via CSV?

Yes. You can upload a spreadsheet (CSV) with post text, scheduled date and time, target page, and optional image/video URLs. PostPageX will import all rows and schedule them automatically. This is especially useful for agencies managing content calendars across many pages.

Is my Facebook data safe?

Absolutely. We store your Facebook access tokens encrypted in our database. We never sell your data, never post without your explicit instruction, and you can disconnect your Pages or delete your account at any time. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
