Plain-language summary: PostPageX connects to your Facebook Pages to schedule and publish content on your behalf. We only access what you explicitly grant, we never sell your data, and you can delete everything at any time. The full details are below.

What we do

  • Store your posts and schedules
  • Connect to Facebook via Graph API
  • Publish posts on your behalf
  • Read analytics for your pages
  • Encrypt your access tokens

What we never do

  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Post without your instruction
  • Access pages you didn't grant
  • Share tokens with third parties
  • Read your personal Facebook profile

1. Overview

PostPageX ("we", "us", "our") is a social media management platform that allows users to schedule, publish, and analyse content on Facebook Pages. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our service at postpagex.com.

By using PostPageX, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our service.

PostPageX is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Meta Platforms, Inc. "Facebook" is a trademark of Meta Platforms, Inc.

2. Information we collect

Account information

When you create a PostPageX account, we collect:

  • Your name and email address (provided during signup)
  • If you sign up via Google: your Google account name and email, provided by Google's OAuth service
  • Your account password (stored as a one-way hash — we cannot read it)
  • The date and time your account was created

Content you create

We store the posts, drafts, schedules, media files, and templates you create inside PostPageX. This content is associated with your account and is used solely to operate the service for you.

Usage data

We collect basic technical information when you use PostPageX, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited within our app, and actions taken (such as scheduling a post). This is used to improve the product and diagnose bugs.

Payment information

PostPageX is currently free. If we introduce paid plans in the future, payment processing will be handled by a third-party provider (such as Stripe). We will never store your raw credit card details on our servers.

3. Facebook data & permissions

PostPageX connects to your Facebook Pages via Facebook's official Graph API using OAuth. When you click "Connect Facebook Pages" inside your dashboard, you are redirected to Facebook's secure login, where you choose which Pages to grant PostPageX access to.

Important: PostPageX only connects to your Facebook Pages, not your personal Facebook profile. We do not access your personal timeline, friends list, messages, or any profile data beyond what is needed to identify your account for authentication purposes.

Permissions we request and why

PermissionWhy we need it
pages_show_listTo show you a list of Pages you manage so you can select which ones to connect to PostPageX.
pages_manage_postsTo create, schedule, edit, and delete posts on your Pages on your behalf.
publish_videoTo upload and publish video posts and Reels to your Pages.
pages_read_engagementTo read likes, comments, shares, and reach data for your posts to power the analytics dashboard.
pages_read_user_contentTo read comments and reviews left by visitors on your Pages, for moderation and inbox features.
pages_manage_metadataTo read Page settings, category, and hours, and to enable real-time webhooks so your dashboard stays updated.

Access tokens

When you connect your Facebook Pages, Facebook issues us access tokens that allow PostPageX to act on your behalf. These tokens are:

  • Stored encrypted in our database using AES-256 encryption
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Used only to perform actions you have explicitly set up (publishing scheduled posts, reading your analytics)
  • Automatically refreshed before they expire to maintain uninterrupted service

You can revoke PostPageX's access to your Facebook Pages at any time by visiting your Facebook App Settings and removing PostPageX, or by disconnecting from within your PostPageX dashboard.

4. How we use your data

We use the information we collect exclusively to:

  • Provide and operate the PostPageX service
  • Authenticate you and maintain your session
  • Publish posts to your Facebook Pages at the times you schedule
  • Display analytics and performance data for your pages and posts
  • Send you transactional emails (e.g., post failure notifications, account security alerts)
  • Improve our product based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, we do not build advertising profiles, and we do not share identifiable data with advertisers.

5. Data sharing

PostPageX does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

Service providers

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate PostPageX. These may include cloud hosting providers (to store your data), email delivery services (to send you notifications), and analytics tools (to understand how our product is used). These providers are contractually bound to use your data only to provide their services to us.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or government authority, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety, or investigate fraud.

Business transfer

If PostPageX is acquired, merged, or its assets are transferred, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

6. Data storage & security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest (including Facebook access tokens)
  • One-way hashing for passwords (bcrypt)
  • Regular security audits and dependency updates
  • Restricted access controls — only authorised team members can access production data

While we take all reasonable precautions, no system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at security@postpagex.com.

7. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Specifically:

  • Account data: Kept until you delete your account
  • Published posts: Post records are retained for 12 months after publication to support analytics
  • Drafts and scheduled posts: Kept until you delete them or close your account
  • Facebook access tokens: Deleted immediately when you disconnect a Page or delete your account
  • Usage logs: Retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes, then deleted

When you delete your account, we will permanently delete all your personal data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain it longer.

8. Your rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Disconnection: Revoke PostPageX's access to your Facebook Pages at any time, from within the dashboard or via Facebook App Settings
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@postpagex.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies

PostPageX uses cookies and similar technologies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We use:

  • Session cookies: To maintain your login session. These expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: To remember your preferences (such as which page you last viewed). These expire after 30 days.
  • Analytics cookies: To understand how our app is used in aggregate. These are anonymised and do not track you individually across other websites.

We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling session cookies will prevent you from staying logged in.

10. Children's privacy

PostPageX is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at privacy@postpagex.com and we will delete the account promptly.

11. Policy changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, for significant changes, notify you by email or by displaying a prominent notice in the app.

Your continued use of PostPageX after a policy update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
