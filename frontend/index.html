Transparency

What we access and why

PostPageX connects to Facebook using official Graph API permissions. Here's exactly what we request and what we use it for.

pages_show_list
Lists all the Pages you manage so you can select which ones to connect to PostPageX.
pages_manage_posts
Creates, edits, and deletes posts on your Pages — the core of scheduling and publishing.
publish_video
Uploads and publishes video content and Reels to your Pages on schedule.
pages_read_engagement
Reads likes, comments, shares and reach per post to power your analytics dashboard.
pages_read_user_content
Reads comments and reviews visitors leave on your Pages for moderation and inbox features.
pages_manage_metadata
Reads Page settings and enables real-time webhooks so your dashboard stays up to date.
